About

I am Alison Zhao and I am currently working as a pre-doctoral research professional at the University of Chicago, Becker Friedman Institute . My research work focuses on political, behavioral and development economics, and I have a special interest in Chinese economy and society.

In my free time, I keep a blog to tell stories with data (opinions and mistakes are my own), and I do photography if I finally get out of the room.
